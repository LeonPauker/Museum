The Evolution of Web Design

The journey of web design began in the early 1990s with simple text-based sites, where minimal HTML was the norm.

In the late 1990s, the introduction of CSS allowed designers to separate content from presentation, offering more control over the look and feel of websites.

Today, web design emphasizes functionality, accessibility, and performance.
